Clips

New York Daily News

I joined the Daily News as an intern in 2013, working out of the Police Bureau in One Police Plaza. I became a freelance journalist for the newspaper after my internship ended, and report on stories all across New York city.

Brooklyn Paper

I interned at the Brooklyn Paper in both 2011 and 2012, a weekly newspaper covering New York City's most populated borough. There, I learned about hyperlocal coverage and engaging with the community as a journalist.

Daily Orange

During my time at the Daily Orange, I was an assistant news editor, the deputy features editor and the opinions editor. The Daily Orange was named the No. 2 college newspaper in 2014 by the Princeton Review.
