Special Projects

"When & Where?"

Syracuse University Crime data visualizations

After collecting data from the university's Department of Public Safety's crime logs, I created a database of the crimes relevant to students on campus. Using this data, I created the maps using CartoDB and the interactive charts with HighCharts and customized them using open source codes. The Daily Orange published three stories on this data, illustrating trends through times and locations.

"A Tale of Two Cities"

New York Daily News homicide data map

I compiled the NYPD's data on reported homicides in 2013, with as much information as I could find. This included where and how the murders happened, the victim's demographic and the investigation status. I interviewed several family members that were involved with these unsolved incidents. I worked on this project with several other reporters and editors.

NewsGiffy

Social media news platform

In 2012, I created a start-up project called NewsGiffy. The concept was to take videos related to the news and create GIFs, and posting them on Tumblr and Twitter. Through this project, I learned video editing, Photoshop and GIF-making skills. I also wrote short blurbs for all the posts. The project ended in 2013.
